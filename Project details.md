# Medicine-Reminder-Health-Tracker



Project Overview

The Medicine Reminder & Health Tracker is a web-based healthcare application developed to help users manage their medications and monitor their health status effectively. The system provides timely medicine reminders, health tracking features, and secure health record management.

This project improves medication adherence, promotes healthy habits, and helps users maintain better control over their overall health.


Problem Statement

Many individuals forget to take medicines on time and struggle to maintain proper health records. Traditional methods of tracking medications and health information are often ineffective, leading to missed doses and poor health management.

The Medicine Reminder & Health Tracker addresses these challenges by providing an automated reminder system and a centralized platform for managing personal health information.


Objectives

• Provide timely medicine reminders.
• Track daily health activities.
• Maintain personal health records.
• Improve medication adherence.
• Support healthy lifestyle management.
• Enhance overall healthcare monitoring.

System Modules

1. User Management

Handles user registration, authentication, and profile management.

2. Medicine Reminder Management

Allows users to add medicines, schedule reminders, and receive notifications on time.

3. Health Tracking

Monitors health-related information such as weight, blood pressure, glucose levels, and daily health activities.

4. Report Generation

Generates health reports and medication history for users and healthcare professionals.

5. Database Management

Stores user information, medicine schedules, reminder details, and health records securely.
